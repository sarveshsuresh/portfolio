PROJECTS

Customer Satisfaction Analysis

Optimization of Fundamental Ratio-based filters using Financial data.

Obtained the financial data (Profit and Loss statements, Balance Sheets and Cash Flows) for all the companies listed in the National Stock Exchange for the past 10 years. Worked on maximizing year-wise returns and the Sharpe Ratio by selecting appropriate filters based on Fundamental Ratios.
